about me

I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Creative Writing from Grand Valley State University. Since 2005, I've worked in a variety of roles - from the guy who whips up your fondue at The Melting Pot, to an admissions representative at a university, and a smattering of other roles in between. All along the way, I've been an avid guitar and drum player. I love writing, recording, mixing, and performing. Most Saturdays you'll catch me in front of my computer with headphones on, sipping a cup of coffee, recording my latest riff or trying to get a mix just right.


Originally from Michigan, I've had the opportunity to live all over the country. I blame my dad for giving me the travel bug; his time in the seminary and as a pastor had us moving quite often. I've moved more times than you can count on all your fingers and toes, and have finally settled in Indianapolis with no plans of leaving any time soon. I currently live on the north side in Fishers with my wife and cocker spaniel, Lylah, and we are expecting our first child in July.


Along with music and travel, I'm passionate about the arts, education, and food. I enjoy reading biographies and military history, but really any kind of non-fiction will do. I have a weak spot for trash TV like 90 Day Fiancé, but nothing is as near and dear to me as Frasier.
